Google Maps Billing

Quick Overview

Google Maps is a pay-as-you-go service. You only pay for what you use, with a $7 USD monthly credit that helps reduce costs.

Free Trial

Google gives $300 free credit for the first 3 months. This covers most usage for small to medium businesses during the trial period.
